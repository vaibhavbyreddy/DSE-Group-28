\chapter{Project Objectives}

Project objectives are specific, measurable, actionable, relevant, and time-bound (SMART) outcomes a project intends to deliver. 

\section{Financial}
The financial objectives focus on the budget management and profitability of the project. The ones regarding the design of the unmanned flying laboratory are stated below.

\begin{enumerate}
    \item The total manufacturing cost per UAV configuration shall not exceed 45k€, including development, materials, propulsion system, avionics, and assembly, as verified at the end of the prototype build phase.

    \item The UAV shall have an operational cost of no more than TBD€ per flight hour, including fuel, maintenance, and consumables, measured during flight testing.

    \item The project shall achieve a positive net financial gain within TBD months after first operational deployment, based on total development and operational costs versus generated project revenue.

    \item The UAV shall be capable of at least X flight hours before requiring a major overhaul, ensuring lifecycle cost efficiency (maybe that is more of a performance objective?).

    \item Critical components shall be replaceable at a cost not exceeding TBD\% of total system cost, enabling economically viable maintenance (can we estimate this?).
\end{enumerate}

\section{Quality}
The quality objectives are used to define standards for the deliverables.

\begin{enumerate}
    \item All sensors shall achieve an accuracy within $\pm$ TBD\% across the operational flight envelope, as validated during flight testing.

    \item All sensors shall maintain a data availability of at least TBD\% during the duration of a test flight.

    \item All sensors shall provide measurements at a minimum update rate of TBD Hz during operation.

    \item Repeated measurements under identical atmospheric conditions shall not deviate by more than $\pm$ TBD\% in measured wind velocity.

    \item The UAV shall achieve a minimum of TBD flight hours between critical failures (MTBF).

    \item Data shall be stored in a standardized format compatible with analysis tools.
\end{enumerate}

\section{Performance}
The performance objectives focus on the results and effectiveness of the project.

\begin{enumerate}
    \item The UAV shall achieve a maximum airspeed of 0.75 Mach for a continuous duration of at least TBD minutes at an altitude of TBD m under standard atmospheric conditions, as verified during flight testing.

    \item The UAV shall be able to sustain an airspeed $\$ 0.75 Mach for a continuous duration of at least TBD minutes at an altitude of TBD m under standard atmospheric conditions, as verified during flight testing.
    
    \item The UAV shall achieve a minimum endurance of 30 minutes at the maximum payload, as verified during flight testing.

    \item The UAV shall achieve a minimum range of TBD km at the maximum payload, as verified during flight testing.

    \item The UAV shall be capable of carrying a minimum payload of 5 kg with a minimum volume capacity of 5 L within the designated payload bay.

    \item The UAV shall not exceed an altitude of TBD m during its operation.

\end{enumerate}
